Keck Science Meeting, 2021

Hello! Below is an interactive poster for the Keck Science Meeting 2021.

As some of these data are proprietary, I've added a faint "preliminary" over the relevant images of the data. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at aibhleog@tamu.edu

How to Navigate the Interactive Poster

Press the "Present" button below to see the full poster.

scrolling around

    • Move your mouse around and zoom in and out to go to the different levels.

manually move around

    • To look at a particular section, click on the circle describing that section. The poster will zoom in for you.
    • To go up one level, click the ⤴ arrow.
    • To return to the full view of the poster, click the 🏠 button
    • You can also use the < and > buttons to walk through the poster.

PhD Writing Prompts

After a writing workshop that a colleague and I attended, I wanted to make a list of writing prompts to help me overcome my scientific writers block. Using some of the prompts that were given at that workshop and then scouring the internet for more exmaples, I compiled a list and wrote a quick script to randomly print one. Integrating this script as an alias in my terminal means that I can type "writing" in my terminal and get a prompt!
